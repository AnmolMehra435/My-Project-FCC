Ferrari SF90 Stradale

The best car Ferrari ever made?

Here are some intersting facts about this machine.

  • Hybrid Powertrain: The SF90 Stradale is Ferrari’s first plug-in hybrid sports car. It combines a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with three electric motors, producing a combined 986 horsepower. This setup enables impressive performance while also improving efficiency.
  • Acceleration: With its powerful hybrid system, the SF90 Stradale can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.5 seconds. This makes it one of the fastest production cars in the world.
  • Electric-Only Mode: It features an all-electric driving mode, allowing the car to operate solely on electric power for short distances. This mode is particularly useful for quiet urban driving and reduces emissions during city commutes.
  • Advanced Aerodynamics: The SF90 Stradale boasts a highly advanced aerodynamic design, including active aerodynamic components like adjustable rear spoilers and underbody diffusers. This design enhances downforce and stability at high speeds.
  • Sophisticated Suspension: The car uses Ferrari’s latest suspension technology, including a double-wishbone front suspension and a multi-link rear suspension, both equipped with adaptive dampers. This setup provides exceptional handling and ride comfort.
  • Innovative Interior: The interior of the SF90 Stradale is designed with a futuristic approach, featuring a digital instrument cluster and a centrally mounted touchscreen interface. The driver’s experience is enhanced by advanced technology and luxurious materials.
  • Carbon Fiber Construction: Ferrari uses extensive carbon fiber in the construction of the SF90 Stradale, both for performance reasons and to reduce weight. This lightweight material contributes to the car’s agility and handling capabilities.
  • Limited Production: As a high-end supercar, the SF90 Stradale is produced in limited numbers, making it a rare and exclusive vehicle. Its production reflects Ferrari’s commitment to performance and luxury.
